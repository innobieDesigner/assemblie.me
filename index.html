About App

Our application is all about visualization. You buy your furniture as usual, then download the app and you have access to all of our features. We provide a solution, which shows you in augmented reality how to assembe your bought product.

One of our biggest feature is that you are able to see the parts when they are built in teh bought product. This way you are able to get an idea where that part will go when you assemble the furniture.

You are able to reach this feature by scanning the QR codes stickers on the chosen part. You will find the stickers on the top or the bottom of the part.
